# Planning Ideas Log

## Purpose / Usage

- Capture implementation-discovered ideas that may improve future planning quality.
- Keep entries practical and scoped for planning follow-up, not immediate execution.
- Prefer concise statements with clear rationale and candidate next step.

## Entry Schema (Deterministic)

Append entries using this exact field order:

```md
### IDEA-YYYYMMDD-##
- **Idea:** <one-sentence proposal>
- **Observed During:** <task/feature/session context>
- **Planning Value:** <why this matters for future planning>
- **Suggested Follow-up:** <backlog item, roadmap note, or experiment>
- **Effort Signal:** low | medium | high
- **Confidence:** low | medium | high
- **Linked Artifacts:** <optional repo-relative paths>
```
